# Appsmove — Content & Storytelling Strategy (Doc 1 of 4) — Framework Reference

> Mechanism reference. Unicorn Studio's adapted pillars are in `unicorn-content-pillars.md`.

## Production Model

Two 12-hour content sprints per month. Each produces 2 weeks of content (15 posts).
- Sprint 1 (Day 1): Weeks 1–2.
- Sprint 2 (Day 2): Weeks 3–4.
- Output: 30 pieces/month, 1 post per day, 7 days/week, fully written + designed + scheduled.

## The 5 Content Pillars (Appsmove's, adapt for Unicorn)

| Pillar | Why it works |
|---|---|
| Behind the Build | Shows process and quality |
| Agency Pain Points / Buyer Pain | Positions you as someone who gets their world |
| Partner Wins / Client Wins | Social proof without naming names |
| Industry POV | Thought leadership |
| The Human Side | Makes you relatable |

**Pillar Rotation**: Hit each pillar 3–6x per month. Across 30 posts, each appears 5–6 times.

## The 6 Content Types (the angle)

1. **Contrarian** — position most disagree with. Sparks debate.
2. **Curiosity Gap** — open loop reader needs closed.
3. **Number/Revenue** — lead with a specific number.
4. **Story Open** — drop reader mid-scene.
5. **Confession** — admit a mistake or weakness.
6. **Direct Address** — speak to one segment.

## The 3 Hook Styles (the delivery)

- **Pattern Interrupt** — breaks scrolling rhythm. Test: could this hook start 10 other posts? If yes, not pattern interrupt.
- **Specific Tension** — names concrete conflict with stakes.
- **Implicit Promise** — signals payoff without spelling it out.

## How They Stack

Every post = Pillar + Content Type + Hook Style.

Worked example: `[Agency Pain Points • Contrarian • Pattern Interrupt]` "Hiring an in-house developer is the worst investment most small agencies make."

## Post Mix (LinkedIn, 30/month)

| Format | Per month | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Story Posts | 8–10 | Trust, personal connection |
| Contrarian / Hot Takes | 6–8 | Spark debate, show conviction |
| Trend Commentary | 2–3 | Stay relevant |
| Myth-Busting | 2–3 | Educate, reframe beliefs |
| Question / Poll | 2 | Generate comments, boost reach |
| Visual Posts | 2 | Pricing highlights, margin math |
| LinkedIn Carousels | 6–8 | Deep dives, walkthroughs |

## The 5-Part Story Formula

| Element | What it is |
|---|---|
| 1. Hook | Bold opening that stops the scroll |
| 2. Tension | The challenge |
| 3. Turning Point | What you did |
| 4. Outcome | The result |
| 5. Takeaway | The lesson |

## The Contrarian Post Formula

| Element | What it is |
|---|---|
| 1. Provocative Claim | Bold statement most disagree with |
| 2. Common View | Acknowledge why people believe the opposite |
| 3. Counter-Evidence | Real data, experience, or logic |
| 4. Reframe | The smarter way to see it |
| 5. Invitation | Open for discussion |

## Trend Commentary Formula

Trend → Mainstream Reaction → What You See → Your Take → CTA

## Myth-Busting Formula

Myth → Origin → Reality → Reframe

## Lead Magnet System (3 types, 1 per quarter)

- **Checklist/Framework** — top of funnel. 1–2 page PDF, easy to share.
- **Process/SOP** — middle. Detailed walkthrough, gated by email.
- **Audit/Strategy Call** — bottom. Calendly + qualifier form.

> Checklists build the list. SOPs build the trust. Audits close the deal.

## 12-Hour Sprint Structure

| Hour | Activity |
|---|---|
| 1 | Review last 2 weeks' metrics. Map 15 posts across pillars/formats |
| 2–3 | Write 4–5 story posts |
| 4 | Write 3–4 contrarian/hot takes |
| 5 | Write 1–2 trend + 1 myth-bust + 1 question/poll |
| 6 | Write captions for 1 visual post |
| 7 | Write 3–4 carousel scripts |
| 8 | Write FB Group content |
| 9 | Edit and polish (read aloud) |
| 10–11 | Design 1 visual + 3–4 carousels in Canva |
| 12 | Schedule 2 weeks of posts |

## Repurposing Rules

- Story Post → Carousel
- Contrarian Post → Carousel
- Carousel → Visual Posts
- FB Group engagement → LinkedIn post
- Partner conversations → Content bank
- High performer → Repeat from a different angle 3–4 weeks later
- Trend post → Myth-buster follow-up

## Metrics That Matter

| Metric | Target by Month 2 |
|---|---|
| Inbound DMs from content | 5–10/month |
| Post impressions (LinkedIn) | 1,000+ avg |
| Engagement rate | 3–5% |
| Profile views | 200+/week |
| Carousel saves | 20+ per carousel |
| Contrarian post comments | 10+ per post |
| Connection requests received | 10–20/week |

> The metric that matters most is inbound DMs.
