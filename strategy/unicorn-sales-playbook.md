# Unicorn Studio — Sales Playbook

Direct-to-client adaptation of the ACA framework from Alex Hormozi's $100M Leads.

## ACA (Acknowledge → Compliment → Ask)

Every message in every channel uses this structure:

1. **Acknowledge** — Specific reference to them. Their post, their product, their headline, their recent hire/launch/win. Proves you actually looked.
2. **Compliment** — Tie to a positive trait: clarity of thinking, operational discipline, taste, focus. Not flattery — recognition.
3. **Ask** — A question that opens a conversation. Never a pitch in the first message. Indirect ask ("Curious how you currently handle X") beats direct ask every time.

## 18-stage Notion pipeline → what to do at each stage

| Stage | What it means | What to send next |
|---|---|---|
| Prospect | Identified, not contacted | LinkedIn step 1 — connection request + ACA note |
| 1st message | Connection accepted | Engage on their content (LinkedIn step 2) |
| 1st Prospect Follow-up | DM sent, no reply yet | LinkedIn step 4 — gentle ACA bump after 3 days |
| 2nd Prospect Follow up | Still no reply | LinkedIn step 5 (email) — case study + soft ask |
| Lead | Replied to a DM | Open-question DM about their AI/automation setup |
| 1st Lead Follow up | Conversation stalled | Re-engage with sharper question about their stated friction |
| 2nd Lead Follow up | Still warm but quiet | Voice note / Loom — 60s personalized — most powerful asset |
| Qualified | Clear fit confirmed | Propose 20-min scoping call this week |
| Not qualified | Wrong fit | Archive politely; nurture only if context changes |
| Proposal Sent | Written scope shared | Day 3: anchor on their stated outcome |
| Post Proposal Follow-up-1 | Day 3 nudge | Day 7: address the silent objection directly |
| Post Proposal Follow-up-2 | Day 7 nudge | Day 14: respectful close — clarify yes/no/timing |
| Booking | Call scheduled | Confirm + send 3-question prep form |
| First call | Discovery done | Send written scope within 48h |
| Closed without Partnership | Lost | Don't re-engage for 90 days |
| Partnership | Active client | Mid-build check-ins, ask for referral after delivery |
| Lost | Final no | Archive |
| Follow up later | Right offer, wrong timing | Monthly value-add touch — no ask |

## Comment selling — daily parallel activity

Build a Hot 50 list of agency owners / AI SaaS founders you want to engage. On every post they publish:
- Leave a 3–5 sentence substantive ACA comment
- Add a perspective, share relevant experience, or challenge respectfully
- NEVER pitch in comments
- Goal: name recognition before the first DM

## Objections → ACA responses

| Objection | ACA response |
|---|---|
| "We've been burned by AI consultants before" | A: That's completely valid. C: The fact that you're cautious means you take outcomes seriously. A: That's exactly why we guarantee the first project. Built and running, or we work free. Fair? |
| "Your price is too high" | A: Heard. C: You clearly know your margins. A: What's the cost of staying manual for another 6 months? If that number's higher than our scope, the math is straightforward. |
| "We're not ready right now" | A: Totally fair. C: Sounds like you've got things under control. A: Can I check back in 30 days? And do you know anyone else who's feeling the AI gap right now? |
| "We can build this with our team" | A: Sure, an in-house build is real. C: Most teams who try this underestimate the prompt iteration cycle, not the code. A: Want a 20-min call to walk through what specifically blocks most in-house attempts? |
| "Send me more info" | A: Got it. C: Smart to want context before a call. A: Send me a 1-line description of your top operational headache — I'll send back a 2-paragraph scope of how we'd attack it. Free. |

## Daily rhythm

- 8 cold DMs + 7 cold emails + 15 follow-ups + 5 warm DMs + 12 comments on prospects' posts
- Block 4 focused hours per day for outreach + CRM updates
- Discovery calls happen outside the outreach block

## Weekly review (Sundays, 30 min)

- Pipeline by stage — any stuck? Run `/triage`.
- Hot leads — anyone gone quiet? Run `/scan-hot-leads`.
- Partner health — Partnership-stage clients touched this week?
- Capacity — heading toward 3-4/month cap? Pull back outreach or raise rates.
- Next week's top 3 priorities.
